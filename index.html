Our Story

A Tradition of Growth

Clovis is a cultivation‑first brand inspired by vineyards and master cigar houses—where patience, climate, and craft define the character of the final expression. Our heritage is the field: early light, living soil, glasshouse warmth.

We honor seasonality and restraint. The result is a quieter kind of excellence.

The Clovis Method

Outdoor & Greenhouse

Sun‑led growth with glasshouse control. We chase vigor, not excess—letting climate and terroir speak.

Living Mediums

Biology‑forward cultivation with composts and teas. Inputs are simple, traceable, and restrained.

Respect for Time

We plant, tend, and finish at the pace of the season. Care shows in the quiet details.
